Before you start, make sure you have these essentials ready:

  • A scale to weigh your boxes
  • Sturdy corrugated cardboard boxes
  • Clear packing tape (2-3 inches wide)
  • Bubble wrap or air pillows for protection
  • A printer (thermal printer recommended)
  • Label sheets or thermal labels

Pro Tip

Invest in a thermal printer - it'll save you money on ink and produce more durable labels that won't smudge during shipping!

Each product needs proper preparation before shipping:

  • Cover any existing barcodes
  • Poly bag items if required (liquids, powders, small parts)
  • Add suffocation warnings to bags over 5 inches

4

Create Your Shipment Plan

üíª

Log into Seller Central and follow these steps:

  1. Go to Inventory ‚Üí Manage FBA Shipments
  2. Click "Send to Amazon" button
  3. Select products and quantities to ship
  4. Enter your ship-from address
  5. Choose how products are packed:
    • Individual products = different items in each box
    • Case packed = same item in each box
  6. Enter box dimensions and weight
  7. Select shipping method (UPS recommended)

Money-Saving Tip

Use Amazon's Partnered Carrier Program for up to 70% off shipping rates! The cost is automatically deducted from your seller account.

Amazon will generate two types of labels for you:

  • FBA Box ID Label: Identifies contents for Amazon
  • Shipping Label: For the carrier (UPS, FedEx, etc.)

Label Placement Rules

Place labels on a flat side of the box, NOT on seams, edges, or corners. Both labels must be visible and scannable!

Pro tip: Take a photo of your tracking number before the carrier picks up your package!
